About Me

I am currently a fifth-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Industrial and Systems Engineering at University of Minnesota, where I am fortunate to be advised by Professor Zhaosong Lu. I finished my undergraduate study in the School of the Gifted Young at University of Science and Technology of China and obtained B.S. degree in Mathematics in 2020.

I will join the Department of Industrial Engineering and Decision Analytics at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology as an Assistant Professor this fall.
